User Research Plan
1. Research Objective

To validate whether AI-assisted content generation reduces cognitive load and improves drafting efficiency for Arabic content authors.

2. Research Questions

Does AI suggestion reduce time-to-draft?

Do authors trust AI-generated structure?

What parts require most editing?

Are suggested tags relevant?

Is summary quality acceptable?

3. Research Method

Prototype Testing:

Generate 20 articles across 4 categories:

Politics

Economy

Sports

Technology

Manual Review Framework:

Score each article on:

Clarity (1–5)

Factual grounding (Pass/Fail)

SEO completeness (1–5)

Tag relevance (1–5)

Summary quality (1–5)

4. Validation Criteria

Average clarity ≥ 3.5

No fabricated facts

Tag relevance ≥ 3

Summary acceptable ≥ 80% of samples
